Year 1

1 Market Square, Newburyport, MA 01950, November 22nd, 2014. Not a special date in time nor any special location. If you went there now, the resturant has changed, it's a seafood place. But in 2014, it was a Not Your Average Joes and it's where I met my wife for the first time. Of course, she wasn't my wife then, she was just a young woman that I was talking to on the internet. At that point, we had exchanged countless messages back and forth getting to know eachother but now was the time we would meet face to face. As long as I live, I will never forget standing there, in front of the resturant, reading a memorial when I heard an acknowledgment. I turned around and there she was. We went inside the resturant and had our first date.

We became an official couple not too long after that first date. We met eachother's families and did all the cute things couples do when they first start dating. We went out for drinks, met eachother's friends, went bowling and did a paint-night.
