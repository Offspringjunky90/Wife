Year 1

1 Market Square, Newburyport, MA 01950, November 22nd, 2014. Not a special date in time nor any special location. If you went there now, the restaurant has changed, it's a seafood place. But in 2014, it was a Not Your Average Joes and it's where I met my wife for the first time. Of course, she wasn't my wife then, she was just a young woman that I was talking to on the internet. At that point, we had exchanged countless messages back and forth getting to know eachother but now was the time we would meet face to face. As long as I live, I will never forget standing there, in front of the restaurant, reading a memorial when I heard an acknowledgment. I turned around and there she was. We went inside the restaurant and had our first date.

We became an official couple not too long after that first date. We met eachother's families and did all the cute things couples do when they first start dating. We went out for drinks, met eachother's friends, went bowling and did a paint-night.






Year 2

Now it's been over a year since I met that shy, young woman at Not Your Average Joes. We do everything together, all the cute, young couple things everyone does and everything has a story. Take these two pictures for example. The picture on the left is Arielle's first New England Patriots game. We drove all the way down, found parking and walked for miles (and I do mean MILES) to get to security walk-thru. Only to find out that her purse was too big to bring into the game so we had to walk all the way back to the car to drop off her purse, walk back to security and then walk miles just to get to the nosebleed seats! The picture on the right is my first experience seeing Blue Man Group. I've wanted to go for years but never had a chance, so Arielle took me. You're probably thinking "That's a good looking jacket". It was brand new, hadn't had it long and on my other shoulder (the one you can't see) is a nice new tear I got just walking down the street from a fence.
